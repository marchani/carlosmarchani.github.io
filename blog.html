How Do You Create a Windows 10 Bootable USB Drive?

It is generally a good practice to create a bootable USB drive of your operating system.

The following items are required to perform these steps:

  • USB drive with at least 8 GB of free space

Here are the steps for this process:

  1. Download the Windows 10 Media Creation Tool
  2. Double-click on "MediaCreationTool21H1.exe"
  3. Read the Microsoft Software License Terms and click "Accept"
  4. Select "Create installation media..." and click "Next"
  5. Select language, architecture, and edition and click "Next"
  6. Select "USB flash drive" and click "Next"
  7. Select removable drive and click "Next"
  8. Click "Finish"
